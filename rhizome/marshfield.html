Over spring break we've stayed in Marshfield a few times. The first time we were there it was really cold and windy, so we decided to light a fire in the fireplace. Even though we had the flume open, the wind was so strong that it was blowing the smoke back down the chimney and into the living room. We tried pouring water on it but the wind would keep blowing in and reigniting the coals. Eventually we had to call the fire department and they took a shovel and scooped the coals out. We vowed to not tell anyone.

The next day Ava, Charles, and Cass came up to meet us and they kept saying "It smells really good in here, like a bonfire."
